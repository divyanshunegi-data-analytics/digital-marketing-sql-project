# 📊 Digital Marketing Campaign Performance Analysis (SQL Project)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes digital marketing campaign performance using SQL.
The goal is to evaluate campaign effectiveness across different platforms, countries, and industries using key marketing metrics.

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains campaign-level data with the following features:

* Platform (Google Ads, TikTok Ads, Meta Ads)
* Campaign Type (Search, Video, Display, Shopping)
* Impressions, Clicks, Conversions
* Ad Spend, Revenue
* CTR, CPC, CPA, ROAS
* Country and Industry

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Identify the most profitable platforms
* Analyze campaign performance
* Evaluate conversion efficiency
* Compare industries and countries

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* MySQL (for data analysis)

---

## 📊 Key Business Questions & Insights

### 🔹 Platform Performance

* **Google Ads generated the highest revenue** (~22M)
* **TikTok Ads achieved the highest ROAS** (~9.53)
* **Google Ads generated the most clicks** (2.9M+)

---

### 🔹 Campaign Performance

* **Search campaigns generated the highest conversions** (90K+)
* **Top revenue campaign types:**

  * Search (~15.2M)
  * Video (~13.3M)
  * Shopping (~12.8M)
  * Display (~12.7M)

---

### 🔹 Country Insights

* **UAE has the highest conversions** (48K+)
* **UAE also has the highest CTR** (~3.94%)

---

### 🔹 Cost & Efficiency

* **TikTok Ads has the lowest CPC** (~1.00)
* **Google Ads has the best conversion rate** (~4.46%)

---

### 🔹 Industry Analysis

* **SaaS industry spends the most on ads** (~2.35M)
* **Top industries by revenue:**

  * SaaS
  * EdTech
  * Healthcare
  * Fintech

---

### 🔹 Records Analysis

* **Highest ad spend:** Google Ads (~38K)
* **Highest revenue record:** TikTok Ads (~295K)

---

### 🔹 Best Performing Combination

* **Google Ads + Search campaign** generated the highest revenue (~6.38M)

---

## 📈 Daily Revenue Trend

* Revenue fluctuates daily with noticeable peaks and drops
* Indicates performance variability based on campaign timing and strategy

---

## 📁 Project Files

* `marketing_dataset.csv` → Raw dataset
* `analysis_queries.sql` → SQL queries for analysis

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how SQL can be used to extract meaningful insights from digital marketing data.

Key findings:

* Google Ads dominates in revenue and traffic
* TikTok Ads provides the best efficiency (ROAS & CPC)
* Search campaigns are the most effective for conversions

This analysis can help businesses optimize marketing strategies and budget allocation.
